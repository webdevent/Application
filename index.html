Apply for Web Developer

Openning at Wellwash L.T.D

OVERVIEW

The BC NDP is seeking an enthusiastic and skilled junior web developer to join us ahead of the next provincial election in October 2024.
Working alongside our creative and digital teams, the ideal candidate will assist in developing and maintaining our campaign’s web presence, ensuring it is engaging, user-friendly, and aligned with our messaging and goals. This role offers an exciting opportunity to contribute to a high-impact political campaign, supporting a fast-paced progressive campaign with 93 candidates across British Columbia.
Please note:The successful candidate is expected to join our central office campaign team full-time in Vancouver starting in August.

RESPONSIBLITY

  • Assist in developing and updating the campaign’s website and content management systems, including the central campaign website and 93 individual candidate website subdomains.
  • Ensure website functionality, performance, and responsiveness across various devices and browsers.
  • Collaborate with the creative and digital teams to integrate and format text, images, videos, and other media into the website.
  • Implement web forms, donation pages, and other interactive elements as needed for campaign engagement.
  • Provide technical support for website issues, troubleshoot problems, and implement solutions in a timely manner.
  • Conduct regular website maintenance tasks, including updates, backups, and security checks.
  • Optimize website content for search engines (SEO) to improve visibility and organic traffic.
  • Monitoring web analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to track website performance and user behavior.
  • Participate in team meetings to discuss web strategies, updates, and improvements.
  • Ensure the website complies with web accessibility standards and best practices, making it inclusive for all users.
  • Maintain awareness of political campaign regulations and ensure the website adheres to legal requirements.

REQUIREMENTS AND SKILLS

Technical Skills

  • Basic knowledge of front-end web development technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Understanding of web design principles and experience with responsive and accessible design.
  • Familiarity with developing for content management systems (CMS) — Drupal and/or ActionKit experience a plus
  • Ability to work with design tools (e.g., Adobe XD, Figma) to implement design mockups.
  • Strong troubleshooting skills with the ability to diagnose and resolve website issues.
  • Experience with back-end technologies such as Databases, and languages such as PHP, Python, or Ruby.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Experience in digital marketing or working on political campaigns.

Terms and Conditions

Salary: $50,000 - $60,000 per year

Location: Vancouver, BC

Start Date: August 2023

Duration: 6 months
